Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how AAK Nation and the Forge Agents platform ("we," "us," or "our") collect, use, and share information about you when you use our services, websites, and applications, including forge-agents.space.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile details when you connect social accounts.
  • Usage data: How you interact with our platform, including pages visited and features used.
  • Social media data: Content and metadata from connected platforms (Facebook, Instagram, X/Twitter, YouTube, Threads) that you explicitly authorize.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and log data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To automate content publishing on your behalf to connected social media platforms.
  • To communicate with you about your account or our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Social Media Integrations

Our platform integrates with Meta (Facebook & Instagram), X (Twitter), YouTube, and Threads through their official APIs. When you connect these accounts, we access only the permissions you explicitly grant. We do not sell your social media data to third parties.

Data obtained through Meta APIs is used solely to publish content on your behalf and is not stored beyond what is necessary for the automation workflow.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers that help us operate our platform (e.g., Vercel for hosting, Supabase for database).
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Data portability.

7. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted storage of API credentials and secure HTTPS transmission.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
